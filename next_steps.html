Next Steps: If you’re new to Praise, start here!

Curious about our church – or never been to a church at all? We’d love the chance to introduce ourselves.

Next Steps is our way of saying hello and answering your burning questions about Praise Church, God and what Christianity is all about. This three-part series of classes is intended help you learn the basics and connect you to a community excited to share your journey.

CHURCH: who we are and where we're going

Who is Praise Church? What do we believe? How did we get started?

This first class in the Next Steps series is crucial for anyone looking to make Praise Church their home church. Led by Pastor Reg, you’ll learn about our history, beliefs, vision and ministries. It’s no sermon – there will be plenty of time for Q&A.

The class lasts about an hour and a half and is held offsite several times throughout the year.

GOD: his past, present and future story

Who is God? Does he really care about humanity? What’s he planning?

The second class in the Next Steps series takes a look at God’s activity through history – from Creation to Present. We also look at what the Bible says about his future plans. The class is divided into four eras – Creation, The Fall, Redemption & Restoration.

In addition to exploring God’s history, the class examines how we can connect with God and the importance of maintaining that relationship. We’ll look at the disciplines of studying scripture and prayer, examining why these tools are so important to the life of a Christian.

This course is 10 weeks long and follows the small group semester schedule.

YOU: how you're designed and where you fit

How does all of this apply to me? What do I do next?

This third class in the Next Steps series is all about you – really! Beginning with the truth that everyone has been purposely and uniquely designed by God, this class is intended to help you discover more about how your life aligns with God’s purpose.

We look at three personal components: Your passions, your talents and your God given gifts. We believe finding where the three intersect will reveal the way you’ve been equipped to serve others and connect with your Creator.

This course is 10 weeks and follows our small group semester schedule.
