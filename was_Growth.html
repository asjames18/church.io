When starting out on the Christian journey, we expect to be changed from the person we are into who God is calling us to be. The bible is full of references about the growing Christian "bearing fruit", "being transformed" and "becoming a new creation", so continuous growth ought to be the norm - but has it been our reality? Should we be further along than we are?


In this series, we expose common self-constructed obstacles to growth, discover how Jesus simplified the process that we complicate and learn why growth as a Christian shouldn't ever be thought of as optional.
