Welcome to Praise

At Praise, we believe that “church” is not a building or a service. It’s not an exclusive club or place of pointed fingers. For us, church is a place of authentic connection; a body of believers committed to serving Christ, the community and one another.

Our mission is to help you rethink church; to be the church that radically challenges negative perceptions and offers a place where you can safely try again.

Come with your questions. Bring along your baggage. Share your story – and discover God’s power to change your life in ways you could not have imagined.
You’re more than just invited - you’re welcome here.

Pastor Reg Lloyd

Reg is a Southeast Texas native who grew up serving at his local church from a very young age. After graduating from Lamar University with a Bachelor of Science in Social Work & Sociology, Reg went on to earn a Master of Arts in Biblical Studies from Trinity Seminary.

His passion for youth ministries brought him to Praise, where he served as a Youth Pastor for 13 of the wildest, most exciting years of his life. With his roots firmly planted at Praise, Reg was then called to serve as Administrative Pastor for 3 years before becoming Lead Pastor in 2005. Reg is married to his lovely wife Shannon and has 3 beautiful girls.

Student Ministry - FOUR12

Students in grades 6 – 12, this is your chance to come alive and connect in a community built just for you! Our 4:12 student ministry revolves around the idea that even at a young age, students can be the example for others to follow (1 Timothy 4:12). Authentic worship. Relevant teaching. Real community. Come and discover what it means to live boldly, honestly and passionately in Christ.

Small Groups

Christians aren’t meant to go it alone. We need the encouragement and support of a community that genuinely cares. Small Groups are a place where you can ask, learn, share and just do life with a group of friends that becomes like a family.

Learn from passionate leaders in a welcoming atmosphere that encourages questions, inspires exploration and walks with you along your journey to live like Christ.

Worship and
Creative Arts

We place a disproportionate amount of value on facilitating authentic, God-centered worship. While there’s more to worship than just singing on Sunday, we strongly believe in the unifying power of collectively declaring the truth of God together.

We believe that excellence in art and creativity points people to God, the ultimate Creator and master artist. We exist to serve the church by equipping artists and encouraging those who attend to passionately engage with God.
