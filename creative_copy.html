Worship Downloads

There’s nothing quite like joining together as one church, lifting our voices in praise to the God we serve. To help carry that worship beyond Sunday, we’re providing a collection of songs for download that have been particularly meaningful to our church. We hope you'll take them with you throughout your day and be reminded of the God who loves you and the community you're a treasured part of.

These songs are recorded live during our Sunday worship services, and while they’ve been remixed for better listening, no pitch correction or artificial enhancing has been used.

UP Worship
Arts Program

Throughout church history, the torch of Christian faith has been passed from each generation of leaders to the one that follows after. We are committed to passing the torch and preparing the next generation well. By dedicating financial and staffing resources, we've developed a program designed to raise up, teach and empower the next generation of artists.

By providing musical training, leadership opportunities and ongoing evaluation, students in the UP Worship Arts program are mentored into leaders who are ready to serve the church with the gifts they’ve been given. For information or to apply for the program, click the link below.
