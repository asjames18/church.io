Wednesdays | 6:45pm

This isn’t just a church service – it’s a family!

Around FOUR12, you’ll hear “Because of Jesus, we’re family” a lot. That’s because we strive to build an authentic faith in God and meaningful relationships with others. This midweek experience is tailored to those in grades 6 – 12, with entertainment and fun, a live, student-led worship band, small group conversations and teaching that’s relevant to today’s world.

JHigh Sundays | 11:30am

Every Sunday, 6th – 8th graders get to experience church in a way all their own during the 11:30am service. Held in the Jr. High Lounge on our upstairs level, junior high students are invited to hang out and tune in with a small-group ministry designed just for them.

Up Leadership Program

We believe young people can do great - even unimaginable - things for the Kingdom. This program was built to mentor young leaders, preparing them for life with leadership training and hands on-experience in the many areas of influence around Praise.

Enrollment happens before every school semester, with leadership training on the 2nd Sunday of every month.

Other Awesome Perks

Being in FOUR12 comes with some serious upside. Check out the cool resources available to students.

  • FOUR12 Room

    FOUR12 has its very own room to hold all of our experiences!  When you arrive at Praise Church, look for the exterior upstairs entrance.

  • the Hub

    Hungry? Growing students almost always are, so FOUR12 has its very own snack bar and café built right into the room! You can nab a snack to enjoy while you listen and learn – but be sure to show up early, as The Hub closes down before the service begins!

  • JHigh Lounge

    This lounge room is for grades 6 – 8 only, no High Schoolers allowed! It’s an incredible, safe and fun environment that junior high students love to kick back in before and after a service.

  • VIP Room

    New to Praise? Prepare for the star treatment. All 1st, 2nd, and 3rd time guests [and the student who brought them] are treated to the VIP experience.  This includes the VIP room with delicious snacks and drinks and an introduction to what we do at FOUR12.
