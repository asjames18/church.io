Plug in. Help out. Change the World.

A church is only as good as its volunteers. From our services to our special events, everything we do is only possible because of the selfless volunteers who roll up their sleeves to make it happen.

There’s a role for everyone and a need for every skill set.
Whether you prefer to be behind the scenes, love teaching children or have a gift for leading others in worship, we have a place for you. With your help, we can run our events, reach our community and change the world.

Volunteer

You’ve got a passion; we’ve got a need. It’s not only fulfilling to know you’ve made a difference – when you put your talents to work, you discover what you were made for. Praise Church relies on our community of volunteers to make our ministry happen.

Not sure where you fit? Let us help you find a place to serve where you’ll feel right at home. We’re excited to learn what you are passionate about, what skills you have and how the unique ways you’ve been gifted can be used to help further the Kingdom.

Outreach

Churches were never meant to be defined by four walls – we’re called to be a part of our community. That means feeding the hungry, aiding the homeless, empowering the forgotten and serving those who need it most.

Whether it’s responding to a crisis or simply being good stewards of our gifts and talents, Praise Church partners with others to minister in practical ways and share the Gospel with a world that’s desperate for truth.
