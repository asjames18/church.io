Rethink

“Church” is a word that divides people. For some, church is a place of safety, support and growth. For others, preconceived ideas or bad experiences have turned church into a place of judgment, irrelevance – or even pain.

We want to change those negative perceptions and encourage you rethink church. For us, church is not the answer, but a place to discover the answers. It’s not a sanctuary for perfect people, but a safe house for the broken. Beyond traditions or rituals, it’s about finding truth that’s relevant to your life today.

No matter who you are, where you’ve been or what you think about church, our arms are open and our doors are wide. Come rethink church with us.

Redefine

Whether it is our jobs, our relationships or even passing trends, we all find our identities in something. Those identities define us, shaping our perspectives and guiding our actions. The important question is, what defines you?

At Praise, we find our identities in God, the one who made us. In a world that changes every day, He is the only constant. We believe our lives go best when God’s truth becomes the guide, challenging society’s ideals and directing us toward a redefined life full of purpose.

Reinvent

The idea of reinventing yourself is not a new one. It’s the subject of thousands of self-help books and the motive behind many journeys. Everyone wants to become their best self and live their best life.

We believe our best lives are not ours alone, but God’s. As we rethink church and redefine our lives according to God’s plan, we are reinvented. But it’s not about being a “better person”. It’s about understanding and applying God’s truth; a relationship that not only changes who we are, but how we live.

That process never ends. It’s an ongoing transformation, shaping us into the people God has always intended us to be. Though we fail, we are redeemed. Though imperfect, we are loved.
